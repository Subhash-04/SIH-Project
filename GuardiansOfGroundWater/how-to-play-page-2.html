Groundwater Management

As the village guardian, you have the following options to keep groundwater levels stable.

  • Use Irrigation: Irrigation helps water crops, but it reduces groundwater levels.
  • Build Recharge Structure: Build structures like check dams to increase groundwater levels.
  • Water-Saving Techniques: Slightly increase groundwater levels by conserving resources.
  • Test Water Quality: Check whether the water is clean or polluted.
  • Add Polluted Chemicals: Introduces harmful chemicals to lower water quality.
  • Clean Water: Implement filtration systems to clean polluted water.
  • Manage Crops: Manage your crops effectively to avoid wasting water.

NPC Interactions

Throughout the game, you'll interact with NPCs who provide guidance on managing water resources. Pay attention to their advice!
