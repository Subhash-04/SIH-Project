Guardians of Groundwater: A Water Conservation Adventure

"Guardians of Groundwater" is an educational game designed to help kids understand the importance of managing water resources, keeping groundwater safe, and making environmentally responsible decisions.

Objective of the Game

Your mission is to ensure the sustainability of your village by managing its groundwater effectively. The village relies on groundwater for drinking, farming, and other daily activities.

Starting the Game

  • Enter Your Name: First, input the name of your character.
  • Select Gender: Choose a male or female avatar to represent yourself in the game.
  • Select a Village: Pick from three available villages, each with unique backgrounds and characteristics.
